Latest news and announcements from our school community.

MAC Football Team finished 3rd during the ISAA football event. The team showcased exceptional skill and resilience throughout the tournament. According to team captain Timmy Mariano, “We gave our best on the field, facing tough opponents and growing stronger with each game. This was a testament to our hard work and dedication.” Timmy emphasized that the team's focus wasn’t just on winning but on building camaraderie and sportsmanship. The players are now more motivated than ever to train harder for next year’s season and aim for the championship.
